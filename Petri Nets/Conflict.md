```ad-important
Let $N= (P, T, F, M_0)$ be an elementary system net. Nodes $x_1, x_1 \in P \cup T$ are in conflict, denoted $x_1 \# x_2$, if there exist distinct $t_1, t_2 \in T:$ $${}^\circ t_1 \cap {}^\circ t_2 \neq \emptyset \land (t_1, x_1) \in F^* \land (t_2, x_2) \in F^*$$ where $F^*$ denotes the reflexive-transitive closure of $F$.
``` 


```ad-info
A node $x \in P \cup T$ is in self-conflict whenever $x \# x$
```
